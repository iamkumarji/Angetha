Hello There

We Are Angetha AgroTech

Angetha AgroTech is a start-up incubated at Delhi Technological University that focuses on using low THC Cannabis (Hemp) to make finished products for people and businesses alike and help them reduce their carbon footprint by using these products as an alternative. Cannabis as a crop absorbs more Carbon dioxide per acre than any other plant effectively making finished products made from Cannabis carbon neutral in nature.

Low THC Cannabis (Hemp) is a farmer friendly crop and takes less time, space, water to grow and requires minimal to no use of pesticides. Different parts of the crop are used to make a lot of products which have applications in versatile industries, some of them being clothing and textile, building material, paper, biofuel, healthcare & nutrition.

What We Do

Angetha AgroTech has its presence in several industries under different brand names.

Carbon-O

Carbon-O is a hemp based premium bedsheets and linens brand that makes high quality linens which last longer and are sustainable in nature. To know more visit www.carbono.in

Gaanj, Switch to Green

Gaanj is a sustainable apparels and accessories brand which makes all its products mainly using low-THC Cannabis and other plant-based materials. Our tagline “Switch to Green” urges the people to consciously decide to indulge in nature and forget our man-made nurture, when you go for Gaanj you switch to a cleaner, greener life for yourself and every being around you. To know more about our products, log on to www.goforgaanj.com
